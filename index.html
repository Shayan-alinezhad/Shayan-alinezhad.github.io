Hello, I'm Shayan Alinezhad, also known as Cloner. I'm a passionate full-stack developer and programming instructor from Mazandaran, Iran.

Currently studying Computer Science , I focus on creating innovative solutions and sharing knowledge with others.

Teaching is my main focus and profession at Nikan Programming Institute, where I help students master programming skills.
